About MINDAEYE

Revolutionizing healthcare diagnostics with advanced AI technology

Our Mission

MINDAEYE is dedicated to revolutionizing medical diagnostics through cutting-edge artificial intelligence. We aim to provide fast, accurate, and accessible medical image analysis to improve patient outcomes worldwide. Our platform bridges the gap between advanced technology and healthcare professionals, enabling earlier detection and more precise treatment plans.

We believe that by combining the power of deep learning with medical expertise, we can transform the diagnostic process and make quality healthcare more accessible to all. Our goal is to become a trusted AI partner for healthcare providers globally, helping them deliver better care through enhanced diagnostic capabilities.

Our Technology

MINDAEYE leverages state-of-the-art deep learning algorithms trained on extensive datasets of medical images. Our primary focus is on diagnosing conditions through specialized neural network models, including:

Diabetic Retinopathy Detection

Our EfficientNet-based model analyzes retinal images to classify diabetic retinopathy into multiple severity levels: No DR, Mild, Moderate, Severe, and Proliferative DR. Early detection enables timely intervention to prevent vision loss.

Recommended specialist: Ophthalmologist

Brain Tumor Classification

Our neural network can identify and classify brain tumors from MRI scans into multiple categories: Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary tumors, or No tumor present. This assists radiologists and neurologists in making more accurate diagnoses.

Recommended specialist: Neurologist

Our models achieve performance comparable to specialist physicians but can process images in seconds, potentially reducing diagnostic delays and helping prioritize urgent cases. All predictions come with detailed reports that can be downloaded and shared with healthcare providers.
